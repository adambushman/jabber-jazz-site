Our Jabber Jaw

The man behind the curtain is Adam Bushman.

Adam started Jabber Jazz in 2018 after seeing a need for more fan-driven, Utah Jazz content. He brings a focus on the numbers side of the game while making them fun and interesting.

Adam earned his BS in Data Science and Statistics and works as a Data Analyst for a local outdoor gear company. He also contributes regularly for SLC Dunk.
